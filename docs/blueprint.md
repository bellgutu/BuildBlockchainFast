# **App Name**: Blockchain Launchpad

## Core Features:

- Hero Section with Consultation CTA: Visually striking hero section with a clear headline, subheadline, and prominent 'Book Free Consultation' button.
- Case Study Showcase: Display of three compelling case studies (Proof Ledger, DeFi Trading Suite, Lux Vault) with direct links to live demos, showcasing the founder's proven experience.
- AI-Powered Development Explanation: Detailed explanation of the AI-powered development process, highlighting the speed and efficiency of the approach.
- Comparison Table: A clear comparison table contrasting the AI-powered approach with traditional developers, emphasizing the time, cost, and risk benefits.
- Calendly Booking Integration: Seamless Calendly integration to allow visitors to easily book consultations. Offer a clear date to highlight the available slot dynamically, which will act as a tool to give reasoning when choosing slots.
- FAQ Accordion: Comprehensive FAQ section addressing common questions and concerns, providing further assurance to potential clients.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Electric blue (#3B82F6), for energy and trustworthiness, but brighter than specified, to compensate for dark scheme.
- Background color: Near-black (#111827) provides contrast and sophistication.
- Accent color: Analogous purple (#8B5CF6) for highlighting key elements.
- Headline and body font: 'Inter', a sans-serif, for clarity and a modern aesthetic.
- Use custom, professional icons for each section (process, comparison, pricing) to enhance visual appeal.
- Mobile-first, responsive layout ensuring optimal viewing experience across all devices.
- Subtle hover effects on case study cards and buttons to create interactivity without overwhelming the user.
